# Product / 产品

## Register / 注册类型

product

产品工具界面

## Users / 用户

TasteLab AI serves culinary R&D leaders, executive chefs, and product teams at multi-location restaurant groups. They use the product while developing new dishes, converting existing chef recipes into repeatable production methods, and localizing dishes for new regions.

TasteLab AI 服务于多门店餐饮集团的菜品研发负责人、行政总厨和产品团队。他们会在开发新菜、把现有厨师菜谱转化为可复制生产方法、以及为不同地区做本地化适配时使用这款产品。

## Product Purpose / 产品目的

The product turns dish ideas, chef notes, and localization goals into testable robot-executable cooking blueprints. Success means a team can move from a dish concept to a reviewed prototype with clear heat curves, ingredient timing, batch constraints, sensory targets, and expert feedback.

产品把菜品创意、厨师笔记和本地化目标转化为可测试的机器人可执行烹饪蓝图。成功标准是团队能从一道菜的概念推进到已评审的打样版本，并拥有清晰的火候曲线、投料节奏、批量限制、感官目标和专家反馈。

## Brand Personality / 品牌性格

Graphic, culinary, precise. The interface should feel like Botinkit's food-tech world: black-and-Calendula contrast, pixelated computational food motifs, dark carbon instrumentation, warm ingredient surfaces, and enough real food texture to stay grounded in cooking rather than generic software.

图形化、有烹饪感、精确克制。界面应该呈现 Botinkit 的食物科技世界：黑色与金盏花黄形成强对比，像素化的计算食物图形、深色碳感仪器界面、温暖的食材表面，以及足够真实的食物质感，让产品始终站在烹饪现场，而不是变成通用软件。

## Anti-references / 反向参考

Do not make it feel like a generic AI chat app, a cold white lab, a dark command-center dashboard, a simple recipe library, or a normal restaurant cost-management backend. Avoid coral/purple AI palettes, vague AI magic, and dashboard cards that could belong to any SaaS product.

不要让它像普通 AI 聊天工具、冰冷白色实验室、深色运营大屏、简单菜谱库，或常规餐饮成本后台。避免珊瑚色和紫色系 AI 套路、含糊的 AI 魔法感，以及任何套在普通 SaaS 上也成立的仪表盘卡片。

## Design Principles / 设计原则

1. Chef control over automation: AI proposes, chefs and experts approve.
   主厨掌控自动化：AI 负责提出建议，主厨和专家负责确认。
2. Blueprint over brainstorm: every AI output must move toward a testable cooking method.
   蓝图优先于头脑风暴：每一次 AI 输出都要推进到可测试的烹饪方法。
3. Private by default, collaborative by choice: customers can keep R&D isolated or invite Botinkit experts.
   默认私有，按需协作：客户可以保持研发空间隔离，也可以邀请 Botinkit 专家参与。
4. Culinary evidence over generic metrics: show taste notes, heat behavior, batch limits, and regional constraints.
   烹饪证据优先于泛化指标：展示风味记录、火候表现、批量边界和地区限制。
5. Bilingual without friction: Chinese and English must fit naturally in dense product UI.
   双语无摩擦：中文和英文都要能自然适配高密度产品界面。

## Accessibility & Inclusion / 可访问性与包容性

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic controls, reduced-motion support, and touch targets of at least 44px where the interface may be used on tablets. Critical status, privacy, and review states must not rely on color alone.

目标满足 WCAG AA 对比度，提供清晰的键盘焦点、语义化控件、减少动态效果支持，并在可能用于平板的界面中保持至少 44px 的触控目标。关键状态、隐私状态和审核状态不能只依赖颜色表达。
