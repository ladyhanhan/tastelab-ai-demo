# AI Agent Liquid Glass Opening Design

## Goal

Create a standalone Remotion opening animation for an AI agent website. The video should feel premium, technical, and aligned with the existing Botinkit / TasteLab AI visual language.

## Output

- Format: Remotion composition
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Duration: 6 seconds
- Background: black
- Primary material: liquid glass with transparent highlights, soft distortion, and reflective edges

## Visual Direction

The revised direction is inspired by Kima Network's first-screen material language: a clean black field, luminous dotted signal fields, transparent orbit lines, and a glassy central object. The animation should feel like a camera slowly revealing a premium AI system object rather than an explanatory AI diagram.

The old grid, halftone texture, dense node network, loading line, and stacked logo treatments are removed. A transparent glass core and Botinkit/TasteLab-style parameter building blocks gather into the frame. The blocks should feel closer to AI agent, recipe, heat, routing, vision, and taste-control modules than to generic glossy cylinders. The Botinkit logo appears once on a clean black area, followed by a minimal TasteLab AI title.

## Timing

1. 0.0-1.4s: glass core, dotted signal field, and subtle glow emerge directly on black.
2. 0.6-2.5s: Botinkit/TasteLab parameter blocks gather around the glass core with a slow camera push.
3. 2.5-4.8s: the material cluster drifts and reveals layered glass highlights, internal lens bubbles, glow points, and front/back transparent orbit lines.
4. 4.8-6.0s: the logo and TasteLab AI title resolve and hold.

## Assets

Reuse existing project assets where possible:

- `assets/botinkit-logo-reverse.png`
- `OPPOSans/` local font files

## Success Criteria

- Remotion Studio can preview the composition.
- The video uses Remotion frame-driven animation, not CSS keyframes or `useFrame()`.
- The background stays clean and premium, with no visible grid, halftone, or noisy texture.
- Text is limited to a minimal brand/title lockup.
- The logo appears once and is not placed inside a glowing badge, white tile, or layered background.
- No opening loading line appears before the main material reveal.
- The main 3D modules read as Botinkit/TasteLab AI-agent building blocks, not Lusion-like glossy cylinder forms.
- The glass and line texture includes a refined dotted signal field, thick transparent shell, internal lens/glint detail, and multi-layer transparent orbit depth.
- The end frame clearly shows the AI agent website opening identity.
- Existing static website files remain unchanged.
